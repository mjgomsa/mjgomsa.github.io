Bar Brawl

November 2022



Bar Brawl is a multi-player video game made using the p5.js and p5.party libraries.

This was a group assignment for my Multiplayer Game Development class at Parsons. Our professor tasked my partners—Rand Rivera and Joshua Davison— and I with developing a game that fit the phrase "the more, the merrier". And we came up with a reimagined brawler game—in which the heroines are four friends who attack creepy guys at a bar. Below you'll find the game and the sprite sheets.

Instructions:
Move by using the A, S, W, D keys and use the 'K' key to attack.
