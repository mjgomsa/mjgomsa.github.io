Who am I?

MJ Gomez-Saavedra

MJ is a computer scientist, web designer and artist from San José, Costa Rica. At sixteen, she moved to the United States to further her education at The Lawrenceville School. While in boarding school, MJ became fascinated with the arts and sciences. Upon graduation, MJ enrolled in the University of North Carolina—Chapel Hill where she currently is a senior pursuing a Bachelors of Science in Computer Science as well as minor in Studio Art.
Both at Lawrenceville and at UNC, she has holistically approached Science, Engineering, Design and Art to find a common ground between her two great passions.

What is this page for?

The Walking Seminar.

A tier-three art class offered by Professor Mario Marzan, The Walking Seminar is "a class that’s indicative of the wider proliferation of participatory and social based practices that the late 20th century and onward re-introduced to artistic production". This class is focused on the beauty and medidative properties of walking as an artform.
