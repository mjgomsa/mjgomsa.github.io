ABOUT

Hi There!

My name is MJ Gomez-Saavedra.

I am a computer scientist, designer and artist focused on creative coding, AR/VR and web development.
My work holistically approaches Science, Engineering, Fashion and Art to find a common ground between my two great passions: Tech and Design.
Topics that I'm interested in are game design, app development and fashion-tech integrations.

Bio

I was born in San José, Costa Rica. At sixteen, I moved to the United States to further my education at The Lawrenceville School. While in boarding school, I became fascinated with the arts and sciences. Upon graduation, I enrolled in the University of North Carolina—Chapel Hill where earned a Bachelors of Science in Computer Science and a minor in Studio Art.
I am currently an MFA Design and Technology candidate at Parsons School of Design.
